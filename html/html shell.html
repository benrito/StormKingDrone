STORM KING DRONE TOUR

Storm King Art Center is an open-air museum located one hour north of New York City, in the lower Hudson Valley. Its 500-acre landscape contains a collection of more than 100 sculptures created by modern and contemporary artists.

This project offers a never-before-seen aerial perspective of some of the most emblematic pieces of the park. Click on each of the selected pieces to enjoy a bird's-eye view of the sculpture and its sorrounding landscape.
